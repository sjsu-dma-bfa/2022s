Blockz

Zakary Portusach

Artist Statement

https://newart.city/space/edit/testing24

"Blockz" is an immersive audiovisual experience that goes beyond traditional boundaries, meshing electronic music with digital media to create an audio reactive journey through 3D environments created with Blender. Through this piece, the artist explores the fusion of two distinct realms within the digital landscape, showcasing the transformative power of collaboration between different fields of creative expression.In "Blockz," the artist explores the lively realms of dubstep and riddim, genres rooted in personal experiences and community bonds. Within this vibrant musical community, each track holds its own essence, enabling artists to craft unique moments by blending multiple songs simultaneously, fostering a close connection between artist and audience.Inspired by immersive music, "Blockz" visually captures its essence. Using intricately designed 3D environments, it invites viewers on a sensory journey where dubstep and riddim is blended with stunning visuals, creating an immersive experience of sight and sound.As audiences engage with "Blockz," they're drawn into an immersive experience where digital mediums converge, showcasing the power of collaboration. Through the seamless integration of diverse elements, this piece forms a unified, transcendent whole, inviting viewers to explore the dynamic relationship between music and visuals. By igniting imagination and curiosity, "Blockz" encourages audiences to embark on a limitless journey of creative discovery, unlocking new realms of possibility with each encounter.
