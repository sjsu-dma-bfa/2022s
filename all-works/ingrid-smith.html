A Small Wandering

Ingrid Smith

Artist Statement

This piece was inspired by a cave tubing trip I went on with my family. After leaving the entrance and then being enveloped by this large vast darkness I was overcome with this feeling that I was a little creature in this world. Unimportant and important at the same time and my fellows in the cave were just the same. All different in appearance and thought but all of us caught in this journey through this cave and life together, somewhere between birth and death. My goal for this piece was to capture some of the sentiments I held while I was in that place physically and mentally. To do that this work was created and modeled using a combination of 2d and 3d software and displayed using a virtual reality headset. My hope is that I can share my experience with the viewer and that they get something out of it.  Each creature in this scene has been created to be unique to highlight the diverseness of each life and ways that we all get along. The title a small wondering is meant to represent the journey or the “wandering” we all go through till we meet our end.

Bio

Ingrid Smith is a digital artist based in Oakland, California, specializing in both 3D and 2D art. Currently pursuing her BA in Digital Media Art, Ingrid finds inspiration in the endless possibilities offered by programs like Blender and Illustrator.Her work reflects a blend of technical skill and artistic vision, with a focus on themes such as identity, nature, and the intersection of virtual and physical worlds. Ingrid is committed to pushing the boundaries of digital artistry and sharing her unique perspective with the world.
