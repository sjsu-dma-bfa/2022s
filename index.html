about

CADRE media lab is pleased to announce Byte Me, an immersive digital art exhibition that challenges the boundaries of digital autonomy and invites you to reconsider the way you interact with digital spaces. Using works of varied mediums including digital photography, video, painting, sculpture, and unclassifiable media,
Byte Me explores the intricate web of signals that define our digital existence.

The exhibition's title, Byte Me, is a deliberate play on words that challenges the idea that all digital objects are destined for translation. It raises the question of whether any digital entity can truly be independent. Byte Me’s curation of works invites viewers to engage with each work to consider the impact of digital technology on our lives.

Byte Me warmly welcomes you to engage with the artworks and explore the innovative relationships between digital art and technology. Join us on a captivating journey to redefine our understanding of the world we live in today.
